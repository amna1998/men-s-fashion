Excellence. Innovation. Value. Style.

With over 40 years of experience, Men's Fashion continues its legacy of providing superior fabric, value for money and trendsetting apparel to its consumers. It all began in 1976 when the textile industry had not even flourished to its complete potential, that Men's Fashion initiated ready-made winter wear, particularly knits for men, and children. From then on, Men's Fashion became a prestigious brand and won respect as the master of detail in the realm of ready-made garments manufacturing.

With over 80+ outlets across Pakistan, Men's Fashion stands as an enormous textile giant today."The persistent, innovative capabilities of our designers, skilful craftsmanship of our workers, and the ceaseless efforts of our marketing division and retailers have made Men's Fashion a success today."

From upstream to downstream, Bonanza | Satrangi selects industries to produce high quality fabric, aesthetically appealing designs and intricately needled impressions to stay ahead of the innovation curve. We focus on the suiting needs of the diverse segments of our society. Therefore, we diligently conduct in- depth research and analysis of the ever-changing fashion trends, only to make a versatile variety of masterpieces available to the customers.
